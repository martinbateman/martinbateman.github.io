    Introduction

    In this lab you will be creating a public/private key, a certificate authority. You will then sign other people ºs certificates, creating a PKI.

    Instructions
  1. Using you Ubuntu Linux virtual machine. Install the openssl package
    2. [help]
    Using sudo apt-get install openssl.
  2. Using the instructions from here as a starting point create a CA, create your own certificate and sign it.
    3.  
  3. Upload your certificate to the Week 9 Teams channel.
    4.  
  4. Download someone else's certificate from the Week 9 Teams channel and sign it. This should be someone that you don't mind sending/receiving email from.
    5.  
  5. Reply to the person in Teams with their newly signed certificate.
    6.  
  6. Try using PGP to send encrypted email between you and the person whose certifcate you signed.
